Everything you need about MiGrade.
Welcome to MiGrade! This document serves as a guide to help you navigate and utilize our digital grading system effectively. MiGrade is designed to simplify the process of recording and managing student grades for teachers of Cuyab Elementary School. Below are some key aspects of our system along with instructions on how to use them:
  • User Manual
  • Transmutation of Grades

Migrade System Access and Login

  1. Landing Page: When you access MiGrade, you will be greeted by our logo, signifying that you've arrived at the right place.

  2. Navigating to Login: To access the MiGrade login page, simply click on the arrow button provided.
  3. Enter Your Credentials: Enter the username and password provided to you by the system administrator. These credentials are unique to each user and ensure secure access to the system.

  4. Login: Once you've entered your credentials, click on the "Login" button to proceed. As an additional security measure, make sure to complete the captcha by clicking "I'm not a robot."
  5. Teacher Homepage: Upon successful login, you'll be directed to the MiGrade Teacher Homepage. Here, you'll find a user-friendly interface with menus conveniently located on the left side of the page. Additionally, you'll have access to important information such as DepEd orders and memoranda, ensuring that you stay informed about the latest updates and announcements.
